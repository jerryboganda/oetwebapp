## Full Description — Google Play Store

Master the Occupational English Test with OET Prep Learner — the comprehensive preparation platform designed specifically for healthcare professionals.

**Complete OET Preparation**
Practice all four subtests — Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking — with realistic exam-style content. Track your progress across every skill area and identify where to focus your study time.

**AI-Powered Practice**
Engage in realistic AI conversation practice that simulates OET Speaking scenarios. Get instant feedback on your responses and improve your communication skills with interactive exercises.

**Expert Reviews**
Submit your writing practice for detailed feedback from experienced OET assessors. Receive personalised comments, band score estimates, and targeted improvement suggestions.

**Diagnostic Assessments**
Take diagnostic tests to identify your strengths and weaknesses before you begin studying. Your results shape a personalised study plan so you focus on what matters most.

**Mock Exams**
Sit full-length mock exams under timed conditions to build exam confidence. Review your performance with detailed analytics and compare your scores over time.

**Study Tools**
- Personalised study planner with daily targets
- Achievement system to track milestones
- Score history and progress analytics
- Vocabulary builder for healthcare English
- Exam booking guidance and preparation tips

**Designed for Healthcare Professionals**
Whether you are a nurse, doctor, pharmacist, dentist, or other healthcare professional, OET Prep Learner provides profession-specific content to help you achieve the score you need.
